About

Clinical training informed by research

Santiago Panesso is an international medical graduate from Colombia who earned his medical degree from Universidad de los Andes in 2016. His training has spanned clinical care and academic research, with work in pediatric infectious diseases, parasitology, and ovarian cancer biology.

He is currently completing obstetrics and gynecology residency training through UPMC Medical Education in Harrisburg, Pennsylvania. His academic work has included research at the University of Pittsburgh and Magee-Womens Research Institute focused on chemotherapy resistance and translational therapeutics in ovarian cancer.
